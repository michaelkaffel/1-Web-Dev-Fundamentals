Early Life

Jasmin Crockett was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri, where she quickly developed the resilience and determination that would define her career. The daughter of working-class parents, she grew up with a strong sense of justice and an unshakable belief in standing up for what's right. After earning her undergraduate degree in business, she went on to study law at the Thurgood Marshall School of Law at Texas Southern University, where she honed her passion for civil rights and advocacy. Those early years shaped her into the fighter she is today—grounded in community, fueled by purpose, and ready to take on powerful institutions in the name of fairness and equality.
